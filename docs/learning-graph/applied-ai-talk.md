On Thursday June 4th at 6pm I will be speaking about my project to generate 100 free interactive intelligent textbooks using my state-of-the-art Claude Code Skills.  These textbooks are designed to help any students from around the world get a free education as good as the best high-school and colleges today.
The textbooks are full of MicroSims and feature pedagogical mascots to guide students down a fun learning path.  The textbooks can also be extended using the powerful xAPI interface to provide fine-grain feedback for hyper-personalized learning.

Other local entrepreneurs have also extended the learning-graph centric architecture to provide employee training materials and cost-effective chatbots for customer service.
