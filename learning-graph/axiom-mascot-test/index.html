    Axiom Mascot Admonition Test Page

    This page verifies that all Axiom mascot admonitions render correctly with the owl positioned in the upper-left corner.

    Welcome

    Welcome to Intelligent Textbooks

    Axiom appears at the beginning of chapters to greet learners and establish psychological safety.

    Neutral

    Steady Guidance

    This is Axiom’s neutral instructional presence.
    He provides steady guidance without strong emotional emphasis.

    Explain

    Concept Clarification

    In this mode, Axiom is explaining a key idea.
    This is used for definitions, walkthroughs, and structured reasoning.

    Thinking

    Reflect Before Moving On

    Axiom encourages deeper thinking here.
    Try explaining this concept in your own words before continuing.

    Tip

    Pro Tip

    This highlights a key insight.
    Notice patterns. Adjust one variable at a time in simulations.

    Encourage

    You’ve Got This

    Axiom appears when the material becomes challenging.
    Persistence builds mastery.

    Warning

    Common Mistake

    Be careful not to confuse correlation with causation.
    Many learners make this mistake the first time.

    Celebrate

    Chapter Complete!

    Congratulations!
    You’ve just mastered this section and added new branches to your learning tree.
